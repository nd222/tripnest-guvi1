KARNATAKA

Karnataka, a state in southwestern India, is known for its rich heritage, vibrant culture, and significant historical sites. The state features a variety of museums that reflect its diverse history, art, and culture. From historical artifacts to modern art, Karnataka offers a wide array of museum experiences.

Bengaluru, the capital of Karnataka, hosts numerous cultural institutions. Mysuru, known for its royal heritage, also features several prominent museums.

Karnataka is the only southern state to have land borders with all of the other four southern Indian sister states. The state covers an area of 191,791 km2 (74,051 sq mi), or 5.83 per cent of the total geographical area of India.

Bengaluru Museum: Bengaluru

The Bengaluru Museum, also known as the Government Museum, is one of the oldest museums in India. It showcases a diverse collection of artifacts, including ancient sculptures, inscriptions, and coins. The museum offers a glimpse into Karnataka's rich cultural and historical past.
